Expand Your Career With Us

We are always looking for talent people – our most important source of energy - to join our team. For direct application, you can send your resume to career@vipteklink.com.

Reservoir Engineer

  • BS Degree in Reservoir Engineering or related engineering discipline.
  • 5-15 years of experience in the oil and gas industry.
  • Experienced in classical reservoir engineering and simulation.
  • Experience with East Texas geographic region.
  • Experience with Eclipse, Petrel,and Aries is a plus.
  • Part of a multi-disciplinary team.

Data Scientist

  • 6+ years experience at quantitative analysis, preferably for a web or mobile company.
  • Proven experience leading data driven projects from definition to execution.
  • A fast thinker, multi tasking and highly independent.
  • Python, R or any other scripting language – advantage.
  • Strong communication, organizational, and project management skills.
  • The ability to communicate the results of analyses in a clear and effective manner.
